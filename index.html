About us

Rukungiri Livestock Breeders and Food Security Cooperative Society (Rukungiri LiB4SeCS) was founded by a group of dedicated dairy farmers with a shared vision of improving farming practices. Inspired by the Indian cooperative society model, we focus on empowering farmers by providing essential resources, support, and market access to help them thrive. We offer services such as bulk selling and purchasing, animal feed supply, dairy product processing, veterinary care, and continuous farmer training. At Rukungiri LiB4SeCS, our goal is to enhance farm productivity, ensure food security, and create sustainable livelihoods for our members.
