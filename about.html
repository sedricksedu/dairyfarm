About Us

Rukungiri Livestock Breeders and Food Security Cooperative Society (Rukungiri LiB4SeCS) is a farmer-driven cooperative inspired by the Indian cooperative society model. We aim to promote a collaborative approach to farming, focusing on supporting dairy farmers in Rukungiri District. Established by 34 committed farmers, our cooperative provides essential services designed to alleviate farming challenges, improve productivity, and increase income for our members. The cooperative is built on the principles of shared responsibility and collective effort. By working together, we create a sustainable farming ecosystem where farmers have access to better resources, training, and markets. We are dedicated to helping farmers reduce costs, increase profits, and improve the quality of their products, all while ensuring food security for the community.

Why Choose Us?

Our Mission

To empower dairy farmers in Rukungiri by providing essential resources, training, and collective support for sustainable and profitable farming

Our Vision

To build a thriving community of dairy farmers in Rukungiri, equipped with knowledge, resources, and support for sustainable success.

Our Approach

We take a holistic approach to farming, addressing every aspect from sourcing affordable inputs to processing milk into value-added products. Our focus is on maximizing resources, improving farm management, ensuring animal health, and increasing profitability for our members

Our Services

Rukungiri LiB4SeCS offers bulk selling and purchasing, animal feed supply, dairy product processing, veterinary services, and farmer training programs. These services are designed to help farmers improve productivity, reduce costs, and increase profitability through collective support.
